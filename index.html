KERALA

Kerala, located on the south-western tip of India, enjoys unique geographical features that have made it one of the most sought-after tourist destinations in Asia. Fondly referred to as ‘God’s Own Country’, Kerala was selected by the National Geographic Traveller as one of the 50 destinations of a lifetime and one of the thirteen paradises in the world. An equable climate, serene beaches, tranquil stretches of backwaters, lush hill stations and exotic wildlife are the major attractions of this land. A unique advantage of Kerala is that most of the destinations here are only a two - four hour drive from the other. Classical art forms, colourful festivals, exotic cuisine are some of the cultural marvels that await travellers. Ayurveda, the ancient Indian system of medicine and Panchakarma, the rejuvenation therapy in Ayurveda have also helped Kerala to gain a pan-global reputation as a worth-visit destination. Season never ends in Kerala, thanks to the year-long moderate climate and numerous festivals and events.

DESTINATION

Kerala, often referred to as "God's Own Country," is a captivating tourist destination in southern India. Renowned for its lush greenery, serene backwaters, and vibrant cultural heritage, Kerala offers a diverse and enchanting experience for travelers. The backwaters, a network of lakes, rivers, and canals, are a hallmark of Kerala, where houseboats glide through peaceful waters surrounded by picturesque landscapes. The state is also known for its pristine beaches, such as Kovalam and Varkala, where golden sands meet the Arabian Sea. Visitors can explore the Western Ghats, home to lush hill stations like Munnar, known for its tea plantations and cool climate. Kerala's rich cultural tapestry is reflected in its classical dance forms, vibrant festivals, and traditional art and architecture. The delectable cuisine, featuring coconut-infused dishes and aromatic spices, adds a flavorful touch to the overall Kerala experience. With its harmonious blend of nature, culture, and warmth, Kerala stands as a must-visit destination for those seeking a tranquil and culturally rich escape.
